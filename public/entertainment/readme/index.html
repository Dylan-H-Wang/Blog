README

The primary purpose of this section is that I hope you can have a basic idea about what Chinese fantasy novel is. Does it like Harry Potter which shows you an intensive and attractive wizard world? Or it likes A Song of Ice and Fire which draws the great war happening between the continents Westeros and Essos? By reading this novel, you may have a brand new view about China and be interested in taking a further study of Chinese culture, which is what precisely I want to achieve. To be honest, this will be a large project for me, from the translation part as well as the amount of work (this novel is really thick!!!). Regardless of that, I will try my best to update one chapter per week.

So let's go to read the Douluo Continent and find it out!

ps: I am pretty sure there will be many problems with grammar and vocabularies. Please show your tolerance and mercy, and I will really appreciate that if you can point them out! Thanks!

If my translation for this novel violates your copyright, please contact me, and I will delete them immediately.
